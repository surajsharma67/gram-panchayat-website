Overview

Dhajag (धाजग) Gram Panchayat is a Rural Local Body in Pragpur Panchayat Samiti part of Kangra Zila Parishad. There are total 2 Villages under Dhajag Gram Panchayat jurisdiction. Gram Panchayat Dhajag is further divided into 5 Wards. Gram Panchayat Dhajag has total 1 school. Dhajag Gram Panchayat has total 33 full-time government employees.
